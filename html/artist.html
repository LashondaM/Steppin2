Featured Artists

Cin'Diego

Cin’Diego is an underground Hip-Hop artist who hails from San Diego, CA and resides in Cincinnati, OH. He performs West Coast, Southern, East Coast, and Trap styles to catch the attention of the masses in this genre of music.

Cin’Diego’s delivery has a unique story telling ability, abstracting from him a smooth and mob boss demeanor.

Once a member of rap group “Rango Gang,” the three-man group blended the West Coast, South, and Mid-West sound together to make music the world never heard before. One of Cin’Diego’s major accomplishments was winning the “Yo Atlanta Raps Video Awards - The Music Matter Award in 2019 with Rango Gang.

Cin’Diego’s music content comes from the introduction into a side of his family (three generations) of working closely to the Mafia, looked after by Pimps and Trap legends. He tells the stories for those that gave him the game, so you will hear murder music, street life, survival, and how to gain success. His swag gives way to the West Coast which is what he represents everyday.

Cin’Diego is currently working on his first solo album.

KG Salone

“Music is the fabric that binds the world together." This is the belief of the talented and extraordinary CEO of Diamond Kingdom Entertainment, Kineh Sam N'Gaojia. Born in BO Sierra Leone, Kineh grew up loving music and desires to bring hot positive vibes that will uplift the world, especially Sierra Leone, into the industry.

Kineh, whose stage name is KG Salone, is an actor, model, and music artist with records of excellence in each field. He has pushed through the hurdles in the industry and attained a significant height among his colleagues. Kineh has built his fan base from the ground up, and now has a true and loyal following from regions all over the globe. He has used his natural sound to break through many genres and continues to find other ways to bring people together.

Kineh's endless love and passion for music dominates his interest in both modeling and acting. Although he already has significant achievements in the industry, he is not relenting, and the world has yet to experience the best from him.

YOLO Band

The Yolo Band - ( You Only Live Once) located in Cincinnati, Ohio is a group that consists of six seasoned musicians, invariably performing great melodies together for seven years. This unique band features the dynamic duo Anita Blue and Courtney Hairston..whose voices blend like angels, complimented by an outstanding rhythm section consisting of bass, keys, guitar and drums.

The Yolo Band is popularly known for performing classic r&b such as Earth Wind and Fire, the Gap Band, etc. Occasionally they will add in smooth jazz, neo soul, and even blues and gospel. Most of the members are also church musicians.

Some of Yolo’s most notable performances have been The Jazz in Forest Park, 2018 - 2019, KAO Corporation, Woodlawn & Clifton Cultural Arts Summer Concerts. Also entertaining at local clubs, restaurants and outside block parties due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

The Yolo band crew are looking forward to collaborating with local artists on their first original song and performing at more festivals.
