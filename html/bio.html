Ann Patrice

Ann Patrice is an Author, Music Publisher, Songwriter, and Playwright residing in Cincinnati, Ohio. She is the owner of publishing label Stepin2theglow, helping aspiring artists in all areas of performing arts reach their highest level of achievement.

She holds a degree in Forensic Science from the University of Cincinnati. In her career years she has served in the transportation industry as a driver for Greyhound Lines, and assisted as a cdl instructor for charter bus companies and for the Cincinnati schools system.

In the music sector, she has more than 80 songs published to her label, with one of those songs "Private Party," which was written and recorded by Patrice, reaching number 7 on Chicago charts holding its place for 2 weeks. She also has a radio station with Pandora featuring her EP Album "After Tonight." Ann's hot new novel "The Chronicles of Yori," was released during the summer of 2020 and can be purchased on Amazon. The stage play "Leaving You For My Husband," a script written by Ann will be in production 2021.

Ann Patrice goal is to breathe life into every manuscript, screenplay and song she touches, in hopes of evoking a sense of unshakable drive and solidarity in others.
